Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The Short Version

We don't collect your data. All tools on Chirag Hub process files entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We don't have servers that store your files.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Your files and documents
  • Personal information you enter into tools
  • Your location (beyond what analytics provide)

Note on Cookies: We do not use cookies to track you ourselves. However, third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Information We May Collect

We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how our tools are used:

  • Page views: Which tools are popular
  • Device type: Desktop vs mobile usage
  • Country: General geographic distribution
  • Referrer: How you found us

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We use Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Yandex Metrica.

Third-Party Services

Our tools may load resources from:

  • Google Fonts: For typography
  • CDN libraries: JavaScript libraries for tool functionality
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Client-Side Processing

All file processing (PDF merging, image conversion, etc.) happens 100% in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are:

  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never stored anywhere
  • Processed only in your browser's memory
  • Discarded when you close the page
