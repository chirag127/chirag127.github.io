What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit.

Cookies We Use

We use minimal cookies, primarily for analytics:

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga, _gid Google Analytics Distinguish users, track page views 2 years / 24 hours
_ym_uid, _ym_d Yandex Metrica Visitor identification 1 year
_clck, _clsk Microsoft Clarity Heatmaps, session recording 1 year / 1 day

Essential Cookies

We may use sessionStorage (not cookies) to cache UI components for faster loading. This data is deleted when you close your browser.

Advertising Cookies

Our ad partners (A-Ads) may set cookies to display relevant advertisements. These are third-party cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.
